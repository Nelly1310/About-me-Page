ABOUT ME

Nelly Gogohounga (She/Her)

Nelly is a dynamic and creative student pursuing a degree in Global Business and Digital Arts. Passionate about design and technology, her style reflects "Aesthetic Minimalism," blending simplicity with elegance. Eager to expand her skill set, Nelly is deeply interested in learning more about UI/UX design, web design, and programming. She is committed to mastering these areas to create user-centered solutions and innovative digital experiences.Beyond academics, she enjoys exploring diverse cultural expressions, from dance to fashion. With an entrepreneurial spirit, she strives to use her skills in design and technology to empower others and create impactful projects.

My education allows me to merge creativity and strategic thinking, preparing me for dynamic roles in the digital and creative industries.
